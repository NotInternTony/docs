### Filtering Transactions

The left sidebar provides a set of filters to help you narrow down your transaction list. Use these filters to quickly find transactions by their current status or isolate activity within a specific time period.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/transactions-filter.png" alt="Transaction filters" />
</Frame>

**Status filters** let you isolate transactions by their current state:

- **Started** — Payment session has been created
- **Processing** — Payment is being confirmed on-chain
- **Paid Out** — Funds have been settled to your account
- **Settled** — Transaction is fully complete
- **Failed** — Payment did not succeed

**Date Range** options let you scope transactions by time period:

- **Weekly** — View the last 7 days
- **Monthly** — View the last 30 days
- **Yearly** — View the last 12 months
- **Custom Range** — Pick specific start and end dates using the calendar picker

### Searching and Exporting

The search bar at the top right lets you instantly find specific transactions by **Transaction ID**, **Wallet Address**, or **Booking Reference**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/search-export.png" alt="Search and export" />
</Frame>

Click **Export CSV** to download your filtered transaction data for accounting, reconciliation, or reporting.
