When you first log in, you land on the main Dashboard — your command center for real-time business performance.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/dashboard-overview.png" alt="Grain Dashboard overview" />
</Frame>

At the top of the page, you'll find three key metrics to track your performance.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard/key-metrics.png" alt="Dashboard key metrics" />
</Frame>

- **Total Sales Volume** — The total value of all successful transactions.
- **Transaction Count** — The number of transactions processed over a selected period.
- **Unique Wallets** — The number of unique customer wallets that have interacted with your business.
