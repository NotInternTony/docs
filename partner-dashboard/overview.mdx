## Welcome to the Partner Dashboard

The Grain Partner Dashboard is your central hub for managing crypto payment processing. From here, you can monitor transactions in real time, track settlements, analyze payment performance, and configure your integration settings.

After signing in at [dashboard.grain.co](https://dashboard.grain.co), you land on the **Home** screen, which provides a snapshot of your recent activity, including today's transaction volume, pending settlements, and any items that need your attention.

## Main Navigation

The primary navigation bar runs along the left side of the dashboard. It is organized into four main areas:

### Transactions

View, search, and export every payment that flows through your Grain integration. Filter by status, currency, date range, or amount to find exactly what you need.

### Settlements

Track when funds are settled to your payout account. Match individual transactions to settlement batches and download reconciliation reports.

### Analytics

Monitor key metrics such as payment volume, conversion rates, and popular cryptocurrencies. Use date-range filtering and breakdowns to spot trends and inform business decisions.

### Settings

Manage API keys, configure webhooks, invite team members, and set notification preferences.

## Environment Switcher

Use the environment toggle in the top-right corner to switch between **Sandbox** and **Production**. Sandbox mode lets you test your integration with simulated transactions before going live.

<Tip>
  Start in Sandbox mode to verify your integration end-to-end. When you are ready, switch to Production and generate live API keys from the **Settings** page.
</Tip>
