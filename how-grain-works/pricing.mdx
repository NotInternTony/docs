## Simple, Transparent Pricing

Grain charges a single transaction fee on each confirmed payment. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or hidden charges.

## Fee Structure

| Fee Type           | Rate    | Description                                               |
|--------------------|---------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| Transaction fee    | 1.0%    | Applied to each confirmed payment at the time of settlement|
| Settlement fee     | None    | No additional fee for bank transfers                       |
| Setup fee          | None    | No cost to create your account or integrate                |
| Monthly fee        | None    | No recurring platform charges                              |
| Chargeback fee     | None    | Crypto payments are irreversible — no chargebacks          |

## How Fees Are Applied

Your transaction fee is deducted automatically from each settlement. You always see the exact fee breakdown before funds reach your bank account.

**Example:**

A customer pays the equivalent of $250.00 USD in USDC.

| Item                 | Amount     |
|----------------------|------------|
| Payment amount       | $250.00    |
| Transaction fee (1%) | −$2.50     |
| **You receive**      | **$247.50**|

The fee is calculated on the fiat value of the payment at the time of blockchain confirmation, so you are never exposed to price volatility after the payment is confirmed.

## No Chargebacks

Because cryptocurrency transactions are settled on-chain and are irreversible, there are no chargebacks. This eliminates chargeback fees and the operational cost of disputes — a significant advantage over traditional card processing.

## What's Included

Your transaction fee covers:

- Real-time blockchain monitoring across all supported networks
- Automatic crypto-to-fiat conversion
- Settlement to your bank account on your chosen schedule
- Access to the Grain Dashboard for payment and settlement management
- Webhook notifications and full API access
- Standard email support

<Note>
  **Volume pricing available.** If you process more than $50,000 per month in crypto payments, contact [sales@grain.com](mailto:sales@grain.com) for custom rates. High-volume merchants may qualify for reduced transaction fees and priority settlement schedules.
</Note>

## Comparing to Card Processing

| Feature               | Grain (Crypto)  | Traditional Cards |
|-----------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Transaction fee       | 1.0%            | 2.5–3.5%          |
| Chargebacks           | None            | Common             |
| Chargeback fees       | $0              | $15–$25 each       |
| Settlement speed      | T+1 to T+2     | T+2 to T+7        |
| International payments| No extra fees   | FX + cross-border fees |
