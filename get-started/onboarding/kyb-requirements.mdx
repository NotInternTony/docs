## What is KYB?

Know Your Business (KYB) verification is a regulatory requirement that confirms your company's identity and legitimacy before you can process crypto payments through Grain. This process protects both you and your customers by ensuring compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF) regulations.

All merchants must complete KYB verification before accepting live payments.

## Required Documents

Prepare the following documents before starting your application. All documents must be current and legible.

### Business Registration

- Certificate of incorporation or business registration
- Articles of association or operating agreement
- Business license (if applicable to your industry)

### Ownership and Control

- List of all beneficial owners holding 25% or more equity
- Government-issued photo ID for each beneficial owner (passport or national ID)
- Proof of address for each beneficial owner (utility bill or bank statement dated within the last 3 months)

### Financial Information

- Bank account details for your settlement account
- Most recent bank statement (dated within the last 3 months)
- Tax identification number (TIN) or equivalent

### Business Details

- Company website URL
- Description of products or services offered
- Expected monthly transaction volume
- Primary countries of operation

<Tip>
  Gather all documents before starting the application. Incomplete submissions are the most common cause of delays in the review process.
</Tip>

## Review Timeline

| Stage              | Typical Duration   |
|--------------------|--------------------|
| Document review    | 1 - 2 business days |
| Enhanced due diligence (if required) | 3 - 5 business days |
| Final approval     | 1 business day      |

Most merchants receive approval within **2 - 3 business days** of submitting a complete application. Applications requiring enhanced due diligence may take up to **7 business days**.

## What Happens After Approval

Once your KYB verification is approved, you will:

1. Receive a confirmation email with next steps
2. Gain access to production API keys in the dashboard
3. Be able to configure your settlement preferences
4. Start accepting live crypto payments
