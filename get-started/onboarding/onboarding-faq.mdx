## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does the onboarding process take?">
    Most merchants complete onboarding within **3 - 5 business days** from account creation to processing their first live payment. The timeline breaks down as follows:

    - **Account creation**: Instant
    - **KYB submission**: 15 - 30 minutes (with documents ready)
    - **KYB review**: 1 - 3 business days
    - **Integration and testing**: Varies based on your setup

    Having all your [required documents](/get-started/onboarding/kyb-requirements) prepared before you start is the best way to speed up the process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I re-submit my KYB application if it is rejected?">
    Yes. If your application is rejected, you will receive a detailed explanation of the reasons. You can address the issues and submit a new application from the [KYB Dashboard](/get-started/onboarding/kyb-dashboard). There is no limit on re-submissions.

    Common reasons for rejection include:
    - Blurry or expired identity documents
    - Mismatched information between documents and the application form
    - Missing beneficial owner disclosures
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which countries and regions does Grain support?">
    Grain currently supports merchants registered in over **40 countries** across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. Supported regions include:

    - **North America**: United States, Canada
    - **Europe**: EU member states, United Kingdom, Switzerland
    - **Asia-Pacific**: Singapore, Hong Kong, Japan, Australia

    Check the [Supported Regions](https://grain.com/supported-regions) page on our website for the full list. We are actively expanding to new markets.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I start integrating before KYB is approved?">
    Yes. You receive sandbox API keys immediately after creating your account. You can build and test your entire integration in the [sandbox environment](/get-started/environment-setup/sandbox-environment) while your KYB application is under review.

    Production API keys are issued once your KYB verification is approved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What business types are eligible for Grain?">
    Grain serves a wide range of online and hybrid businesses, including:

    - E-commerce stores
    - SaaS platforms
    - Marketplaces
    - Digital content providers
    - Subscription services

    Certain high-risk industries may require enhanced due diligence, which can extend the review timeline. Prohibited business types are listed in our [Acceptable Use Policy](https://grain.com/acceptable-use).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who do I contact if I have issues during onboarding?">
    If you encounter any problems during onboarding, you have several support options:

    - **Email**: support@grain.com (response within 1 business day)
    - **Live chat**: Available in the bottom-right corner of the dashboard during business hours
    - **Dedicated onboarding specialist**: Assigned to merchants with expected monthly volume above $50,000

    Include your merchant ID (found in **Settings** > **Account**) when contacting support to expedite your request.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
