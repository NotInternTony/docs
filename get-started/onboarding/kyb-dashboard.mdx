## Submit Your KYB Application

The Grain dashboard walks you through the entire verification process. Follow these steps to submit your application and get approved.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Grain Dashboard">
    Navigate to [dashboard.grain.com](https://dashboard.grain.com) and sign in with your account credentials. If you haven't created an account yet, select **Sign Up** and complete the registration form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the KYB section">
    From the left sidebar, select **Settings** > **Verification**. You will see the KYB application form with a progress tracker at the top showing each section you need to complete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your business details">
    Fill in your company name, registration number, country of incorporation, and business type. Provide your website URL and a brief description of the products or services you offer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add beneficial owners">
    Add each individual who holds 25% or more ownership in your company. For each owner, enter their full legal name, date of birth, nationality, and residential address. You will upload their identity documents in the next step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload required documents">
    Upload the documents listed on the [KYB Requirements](/get-started/onboarding/kyb-requirements) page. Drag and drop files into the upload area, or select **Browse** to choose files from your device.

    Each document field shows the accepted formats and maximum file size. A green checkmark appears when an upload is successful.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and submit">
    Review all the information you have entered on the summary screen. Select **Edit** next to any section that needs corrections. When everything looks correct, select **Submit Application**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can save your progress and return later. Your application is saved automatically as you complete each section.
</Tip>

## Track Your Application Status

After submitting, you can monitor your application in real time from the **Verification** page.

### Status Indicators

| Status           | Meaning |
|------------------|---------|
| **Submitted**    | Your application is in the review queue |
| **In Review**    | A compliance analyst is reviewing your documents |
| **Action Required** | Additional information or documents are needed |
| **Approved**     | Verification is complete and you can go live |
| **Rejected**     | Your application did not meet requirements (see details) |

### Responding to Action Required

If your application status changes to **Action Required**:

1. Open the **Verification** page to see the specific request
2. Upload the requested documents or correct the flagged information
3. Select **Resubmit** to send the updated application back for review

<Note>
  You will receive an email notification whenever your application status changes. Check your spam folder if you are not seeing notifications.
</Note>
