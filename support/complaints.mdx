Your satisfaction matters. We are committed to providing you with great service, and that means we need to know when we get things wrong. We want to help resolve your complaint as quickly as possible.

We treat any expression of dissatisfaction with our services or products as a complaint and will aim to respond. We listen to your complaints, treat them seriously, and learn from them so that we can continuously improve Grain.

## Submitting a Complaint

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Complaint Form" icon="rectangle-list">
    Submit your complaint through our **[online complaint form](https://forms.gle/41qKGk1WojkAS8EB7)**. The form will guide you through the information we need to get started.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope">
    For additional questions about an existing complaint, please reach out to **[complaints@grain.inc](mailto:complaints@grain.inc)**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to Expect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Acknowledgment">
    We will confirm receipt of your complaint promptly and provide you with a reference number for tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Investigation">
    Our team will review the details of your complaint. We may reach out to you for additional information if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution">
    We will provide you with a response outlining our findings and any actions we have taken to address your complaint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow-up">
    If you are not satisfied with the outcome, we will explain the next steps available to you, including any escalation options.
  </Step>
</Steps>
