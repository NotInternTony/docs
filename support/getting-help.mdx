## Support Channels

Grain offers multiple ways to get the help you need. Choose the channel that best fits your situation.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email Support" icon="envelope">
    Reach our support team directly at **support@grain.com**. Best for detailed inquiries, account-specific questions, and issues that require documentation or attachments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dashboard Support Widget" icon="message">
    Click the **Help** button in the bottom-right corner of your Grain Dashboard to start a live conversation with our support team. Best for quick questions and real-time troubleshooting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Expected Response Times

| Priority | Description | Response Time |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Critical** | Payment processing down, funds at risk | Under 1 hour |
| **High** | Feature not working, settlement delays | Under 4 hours |
| **Normal** | General questions, configuration help | Under 1 business day |
| **Low** | Feature requests, general feedback | Under 2 business days |

<Tip>
  Business hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM UTC. Critical issues are monitored 24/7.
</Tip>

## What to Include in a Support Request

Providing the right information upfront helps our team resolve your issue faster. Include as many of the following details as possible:

### Account Information

- Your **Merchant ID** or the email associated with your Grain account
- The **environment** you are working in (sandbox or production)

### Issue Details

- A clear description of the problem or question
- The **date and time** the issue occurred
- Any **transaction IDs** or **payment references** related to the issue

### Technical Context

- The **API endpoint** or **SDK method** involved, if applicable
- The full **request and response payloads** (redact any sensitive keys)
- Relevant **error messages** or **HTTP status codes**
- Your integration language and library version

<Note>
  Never share your API secret keys in support requests. If you suspect a key has been compromised, rotate it immediately from your Dashboard under **Settings > API Keys**.
</Note>
